Playing field

The playing field has the following position:

Elements on board are named:

Pawn = SideWall

Bishop = BackWall

Rook = Goal Post

Grid = Goal/Net

This pieces cannot be moved or captured by any player or piece. Walls are different because they can affect the movement of the ball. (Section: "The ball")

Player pieces

Each player controls 2 type of pieces:

2 supporters (KNIGHT = SUPPORTER)

1 captain (QUEEN = CAPTAIN)

Supporters move as knights.

Captains move as queens.

They can capture opponents pieces.

The ball

This piece is neutral. We can use KING = BALL.

The ball moves when a piece captures it. It can move up to 3 squares, where it can bounce along the way.
