Playing field

Elements on board are named:

Pawn/Bishop = Wall

Rook = Goal Post

Grid = Goal/Net

This pieces cannot be moved or captured by any player or piece. Walls are different because they can affect the movement of the ball. (Section: "The ball")

Player pieces

Each player controls 2 type of pieces:

2 supporters (KNIGHT = SUPPORTER)

1 captain (QUEEN = CAPTAIN)

Supporters move as knights.

Captains move as queens.

They can capture opponents pieces.

The ball

This piece is neutral. We can use KING = BALL.

The ball moves when a piece captures it. It can move up to 3 squares, where it can bounce along the way.

From walls it bounces reverse diagonally if bounced diagonally from opponents side else it bounces in any direction.

From goal posts it bounces reverse direction if bounced orthogonally else it bounces any direction.

From captains it bounces reverse direction.

From supporters it bounces in any direction.

The ball is the only piece that can enter into a goal.

Gameplay

Players usually play 50 moves per player where they try to make as many goals as possible. After each goal the board resets. Player with most goals wins.
